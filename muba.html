Coach Mubasheer Joban

Head Coach & Technical Director

Coach Mubasheer brings both competitive skill and Islamic character to Suhbah Soccer Camp, helping boys grow as athletes and brothers in faith. With over 15 years of playing experience and deep expertise in injury prevention and athlete recovery, he combines technical excellence with spiritual mentorship.

Soccer Journey

  • 15+ years of playing experience across the highest levels of youth and adult soccer in Washington State
  • Youth Foundation: Started with Muslim Youth Soccer Club at MAPS Redmond
  • Elite Youth Development: Progressed through top-tier teams including Crossfire Select, Lake Washington Premier A, and Crossfire Premier ECNL
  • Semi-Professional: Played at the semi-pro level with Bellevue Athletic
  • Premier League Experience: Competed in Cascadia Premier League (CPL) with Strangers United
  • College Recruitment: Received multiple Division III and Division II offers, with interest from a Division I program
  • Academic Choice: Chose the academic path at the University of Washington
  • Resilience & Recovery: Overcame two ACL injuries with full recovery, gaining expertise in injury prevention, strength training, and athlete recovery

Character & Faith

  • Islamic Studies: Committed mentor and Islamic studies student
  • Quranic Studies: Studies Quran under Shaykh Nabil
  • Ijazah Certifications: Holds two Ijazahs in Hafs and Shu'bah
  • Leadership Role: Served as Vice President of the Muslim Students Association at University of Washington
  • Mentorship Focus: Dedicated to helping young athletes develop both competitive skills and Islamic character
  • Community Builder: Creates an environment where boys can grow as athletes and brothers in faith
